Shadows Over Camalot

Genre: Strategy

Released: 2010

Description

Shadows Over Camelot is a semi-co-op game in which most players are trying to work together to defeat the game put one player is a traitor who is trying to drive them to lose.

Review

Shadows Over Camelot is a difficult game even without the traitors efforts sabotage the team. It feels like the team is always on the brink of losing the game and the game requires all players to stay engaged and work together to work against the game and the unknown traitor.
