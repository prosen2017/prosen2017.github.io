Dominion

  • Genre: Deckbuilding
  • Released: 2008

Description

Dominion is a deck building game for 2-4 players. Players take turns playing their cards and acquiring new ones to help them best acquire the most victory points.

Review

Dominion is one of my favorite games. Each game is played with 10 of 25 potential cards, so there is a great deal of replayability and each game feels different. There is not simply one strategy that will always work so players are constantly challenged.

Pandemic

  • Genre: Strategy
  • Released: 2009

Description

Pandemic is a fully co-op strategy game in which players attempt to save the world from 4 diseases outbreaks that threaten to distroy the human race.

Review

Pandemic is a great gaming experience, as all players are working together to defeat the game. This keeps everyone engaged as everything that happens in the game affects the entire team. Due to the game's randomized mechanics, games can be fairly easy or lost quite quickly so it is hard to anticipate how a game will go.

Shadows Over Camalot

  • Genre: Strategy
  • Released: 2010

Description

Shadows Over Camelot is a semi-co-op game in which most players are trying to work together to defeat the game while one player is a traitor who is trying to drive them to lose. It is set in the days of King Aurther with each player taking on the role of a Knight of the round table setting off to accomplish quests. The traitor is trying to covertly stop these quests from being successful.

Review

Shadows Over Camelot is a difficult game even without the traitors efforts to sabotage the team. It feels like the team is always on the brink of losing the game and the game requires all players to stay engaged, working together gainst the game and the unknown traitor. Having played the game several times, I much prefer to play as the traitor as it only takes a few small mistakes or unlucky draws by the other players for you to win.
