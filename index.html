Agricola

Genre: Strategy

Released: 2010

Description

Agricola is a worker placement game for 2-4 players. Players compete to build the best farm they can over the course of 16 rounds, which are divided into six phases. Each round brings new elements that players can add, but they must carefully plan ahead by gathering resources to be able to complete their farms. Players must also make sure to generate enough food to feed their family at the completion of each phase. Agricola is an engaging strategy game as players must keep track of what other players are doing since as each action can only be used by one player in each round. This will often require a player to change their original strategy in order to accomplish their goal. Agricola is a good game for all age ranges. The games offers simplified variations for younger players and added complexities for older and more experienced players. I would consider Agricola a staple game in any gamers library.
